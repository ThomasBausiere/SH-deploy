Welcome & purpose

This app is designed to help you build your character efficiently by browsing elite skills, checking where they are obtained, and tracking what your toons already own.

How to use

  1. Select (or create) a toon.
  2. Use search and filters to find skills.
  3. Click “locations” to see bosses and open details.
  4. Use the checkbox to add/remove skills to your toon.

Disclaimer

Some data may be inaccurate. I’m currently verifying and improving it with the help of other players. Thanks for your patience!

Tip: Create a toon first, then start adding skills as you play.
